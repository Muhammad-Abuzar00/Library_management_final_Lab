Add Author:
POST /authors/add
http://localhost:3000/authors/add

Update Author:
PUT /authors/update/:id
http://localhost:3000/authors/update/{id}

Delete Author:
DELETE /authors/delete/:id
http://localhost:3000/authors/delete/{id}

Fetch All Authors:
GET /authors
http://localhost:3000/authors
